## 5. Key levels

**Drawing method:**
"when you're drawing key levels, you want to start off on the daily time frame, then go to the four hour time frame, the one hour time frame, and then finally the one minute time frame." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_2_My_Bread_Butter_Strategy_How_To_Draw_Key_Levels_transcript.txt, [977s-988s])

"I go on the daily time frame, see what's going on on the daily chart. Four hour time frame, see what's going on. One hour, and then I skip all the way to the one minute. I don't go on the five minute. I don't go on the 10 minute. I skip to the one minute." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_2_My_Bread_Butter_Strategy_How_To_Draw_Key_Levels_transcript.txt, [988s-1002s])

**Body vs wick:**
"I believe you should draw it based off of context. Okay? A lot of traders, right, they only draw it on one. So they would only draw it on the body or they would only draw it on the wick. I don't do that. I draw both, right, depending on the context of the trade." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_2_My_Bread_Butter_Strategy_How_To_Draw_Key_Levels_transcript.txt, [1432s-1450s])

**Previous day pivot levels:**
"key pivot levels from the previous day. So from the previous day, when we see a higher low, lower high that has two to three touches, we're going to draw out those key levels for ourselves. Right. Because if it had touches back then, that means there's liquidity within those levels." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_2_My_Bread_Butter_Strategy_How_To_Draw_Key_Levels_transcript.txt, [1025s-1039s])

**Intraday levels:**
"drawing levels as the day progresses... we're simply just going to be drawing the higher highs, the higher lows, or the lower lows and lower highs, depending on which way the socket is trending." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_2_My_Bread_Butter_Strategy_How_To_Draw_Key_Levels_transcript.txt, [1050s-1065s])

**Less is more:**
"personally for me less is more right? So I like the least amount of levels possible." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_2_My_Bread_Butter_Strategy_How_To_Draw_Key_Levels_transcript.txt, [1552s-1557s])

"about five to six levels at most" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_2_My_Bread_Butter_Strategy_How_To_Draw_Key_Levels_transcript.txt, [1568s-1573s])

**Fresh chart daily:**
"I always start with a brand new fresh chart every night" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_3_How_To_Read_Trend_Structure_How_To_Read_Indexes_transcript.txt, [2439s-2441s])

**Pre-market high/low:**
"You can also draw pre-market high and pre-market low as well." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_2_My_Bread_Butter_Strategy_How_To_Draw_Key_Levels_transcript.txt, [1584s-1588s])
