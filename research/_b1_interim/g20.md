# Trading Rule Extraction

## 1. Break-and-retest (core setup)

**Break validity:**
- "the break and retest setup... It works because stock moves up from a consolidation period, it moves up above its resistance or support. It breaks that rate where buyers or sellers are present near those levels because that's where it had a strong break above. And so when it comes back and pulls back into that area, it should have it buyers or sellers, depending if you're going along or short, right, to actually move the stock in the direction that is desired for us." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_6_Part_2_transcript.txt, [1141s-1170s])

**Retest:**
- "the break and retest, you know, you need to wait, right? There's a lot of waiting with the break and retests." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_7_How_To_Play_The_Open_Multi_Time_Frame_Analysis_transcript.txt, [229s-232s])

**Entry trigger candle:**
- "We want an inverted hammer candle off that bare flag. We would enter in off the inverted hammer candle" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_7_How_To_Play_The_Open_Multi_Time_Frame_Analysis_transcript.txt, [324s-326s])

**Stop placement:**
- "your risk level is always the break of key level or a break of trend or architecture" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_7_How_To_Play_The_Open_Multi_Time_Frame_Analysis_transcript.txt, [421s-426s])
- "this is where you would want to put your stop loss, because if we did break below this, right, this stock would have come back into that, it would break that previous high, right, and this is the impulse to move in the impulse of candle that actually broke that new high" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_6_Part_2_transcript.txt, [456s-464s])

**Targets:**
- "high of day low of day key levels" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_7_How_To_Play_The_Open_Multi_Time_Frame_Analysis_transcript.txt, [617s-618s])

**First-break-of-day vs later breaks:**
- "with break and retest, we are waiting for the trend to confirm... So, in today, if you're just trading break and retest, you can't enter into a trade one to two minutes in, because you need to know which way the trend is in today. Right? So, we enter after the trend is clear on the day." (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_7_How_To_Play_The_Open_Multi_Time_Frame_Analysis_transcript.txt, [346s-362s])

## 3. 84% rule / re-entries

**Definition and conditions:**
- "the 84% rule... if you have an entry, like an entry criteria, let's say it's a break and retest rate, a stock breaks high of day, comes back, retest to key level, right? Looking and you're looking for whatever a new high of day or a new key level, inner stop losses at the break of market structure. Let's say you break that market structure, right? But then you come back later on in the day, this can be like 5, 10, 15, 20 minutes, whatever. You come back to that same area later on in the day, that same key level that you first entered on, and you have the exact same trade setting up, right? So you have the same break of that key level, you have the same retest of the key level, you have, you know, a hammer candle still there, you have the same stop loss you could put in before, and you have the same profit target as before. Well, that's called the 84% rule, and theoretically what the 84% rule indicates is 84% of the time, if the criteria is the same as your first trade and the first trade criteria and the system obviously has to be correct, but which is, which ours is, if the criteria is the exact same, 84% of the time, this second trade should work out, right?" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_6_Part_2_transcript.txt, [68s-129s])

**Application after stop-out:**
- "if you do stop out of that trade, you can always reassess and reenter it" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_6_Part_2_transcript.txt, [189s-190s])
- "if this trade ever came back, right, if this trade ever came back here and then retested, right, we could always look at this again, we can always reassess, right, see what the queue is doing, see the context of the market, and we could always reenter, right, for that, for a good trade" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_6_Part_2_transcript.txt, [786s-803s])
