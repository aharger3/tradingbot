## ADDITIONAL RULES FOUND IN THESE SOURCES

### Order Types

**Rule: Market order for quick entry**
"for myself personally right when I take trades I need to be quick... I buy the ask and I sell the bid this way I get in and out of trades as fast as possible" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [486s-541s])

**Rule: Market order fills instantly**
"if you hit market order on your broker when you're entering into a trade you will get filled Instantly" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1197s-1206s])

**Rule: Market order fills on ask (buy) and bid (sell)**
"the market order will do the work for you and it will fill you on the ask price when you're looking to buy the trade and it will sell on the bid price when you're looking to sell a trade" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1206s-1219s])

**Rule: Market order trade-off - not best price but guaranteed fill**
"when you do a market order you're not going to get the best prices. Okay. That's the one thing you have to understand when you do a market order you will not get the best prices however you will be getting filled" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1265s-1279s])

**Rule: Limit order for best price**
"a limit order this is what it's going to give you the best buy and sell price on a stock... this is how you're going to get the best entry and exit" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1294s-1306s])

**Rule: Limit order fills on bid (buy) and ask (sell)**
"the limit order will fill you on the bid when you're looking to buy and it will sell on the ask when you're looking to actually sell the trade" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1379s-1386s])

**Rule: Limit order risk - may not fill on fast moves**
"the problem with the limit order is that sometimes when a stock is moving too quickly it won't fill you therefore you may miss an entire move" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1351s-1355s])

**Rule: Market order recommended for newer traders**
"for a newer trader. I think market orders are just so simple right like you can just do market... most people though just be better off doing market orders" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1234s-1247s])

**Rule: Market order caution for small accounts on wide spreads**
"for some people that have smaller accounts right market order may not be the best way to enter in for some of you guys... if you're trading names with less spread then market order is definitely the way to go" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1518s-1533s])

### Bid/Ask Mechanics

**Rule: Buy the ask, sell the bid for fastest execution**
"I buy the ask and I sell the bid" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [541s-547s])

**Rule: Bid shows demand, ask shows resistance**
"the bid is showing the demand... buyers are the ones that are trying to hold the bid price up... the ask price is this is what showing resistance right? this is where sellers want to hold the price down" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [597s-617s])

**Rule: Smallest bid-ask spread is key**
"having the smallest bid and ask spread is key because the the smaller the bid and ask spread is the less the less likely hood of you losing more potential money on that trade" (boot-camp-recordings_Day_2_Introduction_To_Trading_transcript.txt, [1082s-1097s])
