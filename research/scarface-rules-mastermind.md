- "Premarket high/low from 4AM-9:29AM ET." (mastermind-2-0_1468438, 01:34:08)

**Hierarchy:**
1. HOD / LOD — primary profit targets
2. Premarket high / low — key structure
3. Previous day high / low — separate playbooks for each

5. Psychological round numbers — "psych levels"
6. VWAP — directional bias only (above = calls, below = puts), confluence for stops but never standalone

## 9. Trade selection

**A+ setup criteria:**
1. Strong momentum / displacement on break
2. QQQ/SPY market structure aligned (QQQ broke key level in same direction)
3. Entry level is a HTF level (4H/1H pivot)
4. Clean retest with preferred candle (weak inverted hammer for shorts, hammer for longs)
5. Clear stop — you know immediately where risk is (if unclear, skip)
6. R:R ≥ 1.85 (minimum), target ~2R average

**Regular B&R:** Has QQQ context but missing one of the above factors.

**Trade count:**
- "I can only take 3 trades in a day." (mastermind-1-0_1460324, 00:24:00)

## Ambiguities

1. **"One candle rule"** — term absent from all 28 mastermind transcripts. Only found in performance coaching (pending extraction). Definition from coaching context: J-Dub's B&R variant where entry trigger is single-candle confirmation + order block at key level. Different playbooks for PDH vs PDL versions.
2. **84% rule origin** — stated as "theoretical." No backtest data, sample size, or sources cited for the 84% figure.
3. **Scaling percentages** vary across examples (20%/60%/20%, 15-20%/20%/50%/10%, 50% first scale). Context-dependent, no hard rule.
4. **Body vs wick stop placement** — "depends on context and confluence." No mechanical rule.
5. **Stop to BE** — contradictory rules across instructors. Scarface says never; other instructors (accelerator) say after TP1.
6. **Displacement quantification** — explicitly stated as subjective: "I personally don't have a value." No ATR multiple or body-size threshold.
7. **"Levels are zones"** but zone width (2-10 cents) may not scale with stock price ($250 TSLA vs $20 stock).
8. **Open play vs B&R boundary** after 9:45 — timing distinction, not mechanical. Open play enters before clear trend using HTF thesis; B&R waits for intraday trend confirmation.
9. **When to override QQQ weakness** with individual stock level strength — "trust your key level" — but conditions for when to override vs defer to QQQ not codified.
10. **No HTF entry/stop rules for swing** — confirmed B&R works on HTF but deferred to separate workshop.
