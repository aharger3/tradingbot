## 3. 84% RULE / RE-ENTRIES

### Core Definition
The 84% rule states that if you get stopped out of a trade but the thesis remains intact, re-entering the exact same setup has an 84% chance of working out. It is a re-entry strategy, not a premature exit strategy.

### Key Rules

**Rule 1: The 84% rule is only valid if the first trade would have stopped you out.**
"the 84% rule is only valid if the first trade would have stopped you out" (7kajZjCStT8_transcript.txt, [1351s])

**Rule 2: The 84% rule is a reclaim trade — you enter on the reclaim of the key level, not necessarily on a retest.**
"84% rule majority of the times, right? I'll always look to get in on the reclaim because it doesn't always come back down to retest" (7kajZjCStT8_transcript.txt, [3771s])

**Rule 3: On the 84% rule re-entry, you can size up because risk and reward are clearer.**
"So I took about 60 contracts here, right? So from the first, you guys know how much I took on the first one, right? I about doubled or tripled my size on the second one. Why? This was a better trade for me and the risk was very, very clear and the reward was very, very clear, right?" (Zt8c-5OcLGk_transcript.txt, [241s-254s])

**Rule 4: On the 84% rule re-entry, you do not need to take scales at high of day — there is a higher probability of continuation to the second target.**
"So we're taking a break and retest setup. A lot of times you're going to take some scales at your, your high day, right, this could be 25% or 50%. But if you have in 84% rule and it resets back up, you don't have to take scales at high day because now there's a better chance that if it does hit high day, now it's going to have continuation towards your second target, which will be TP to." (_QNeb-S9xv0_transcript.txt, [1615s-1659s])

**Rule 5: The 84% rule requires strong price action at the retest level, not just any candle.**
"I want to see some strong price action here though, right if I'm entering into this trade on the 84 percent rule I need to see some strong buying action near this level right. I'm not just going to enter off some random candle" (pGLsdLZahVY_transcript.txt, [3230s-3239s])

"the 84% rule is invalidated only if it doesn't break your thesis the first time. "the order blocks are just up close candles" (-k6ayIaeOzs_transcript.txt, [5216s-5217s])
