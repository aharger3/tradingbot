**1. Break-and-retest:**

- The setup works best with a 40-60% win rate and a 1:2 or 1:3 risk-reward ratio. "the break and retest works the best anywhere between a 40 to 60% win rate and you have around a 1 to 2 or 1 to 3 risk reward" (xJ521HB06gg_transcript.txt, [2579s-2584s])
