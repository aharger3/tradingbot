**1. Break-and-retest:**
- The break-and-retest is the primary setup, requiring a clear trend and displacement; it should not be traded in choppy, trendless markets. "if you guys have watched any single video of mine you guys know right if there's not a trend you really do not want to trade the break and retest set up. You're you're just fomoing into to the trade" (fkLwQuAYRdg_transcript.txt, [4417s])
- A break-and-retest can occur across timeframes, including pre-market break with live-market retest. "sometimes a stock and break and retest and sometimes a stock and break in pre-market and then retest in in regular trading hours and still that would be considered a break and retest" (fkLwQuAYRdg_transcript.txt, [4655s])
- The ideal entry is a retest of a key level (previous day high/low, order block, pre-market high) with confirmation of strength/weakness. "Now, when we come back for the retest, Tesla shows a lot of strength in the open. And then on the retest when we're coming back, where do I want it to retest? Well, I wanted the retest of either this order block candle right here, right? Or I wanted to retest of this previous day high, okay?" (ffgXS_5BP9s_transcript.txt, [78s-94s])
- For continuation, the key is a new high/low of day followed by a retest of that level. "We want to see basically what I want to see on any of these names is a brand new high of day slash low of day and then a retest of that level that's going to be the key for me For continuation to the either the upside or to the downside today" (g2EC_kt6G3g_transcript.txt, [880s-886s])
- A "pop and fade" requires the pop to happen first. "For a pop and fade you have to pop first not drop first" (g2EC_kt6G3g_transcript.txt, [3241s-3245s])
- The break-and-retest is also applied to swing trades on higher timeframes. "do you apply the break and retest to your swings? Yeah, I do apply the break and retest those swings. But the swings are a higher time frame pattern. I also have higher time frame patterns on the swings as well. And I'm basically just looking for the lowest risk on those pattern breaks on the higher time frame" (fkLwQuAYRdg_transcript.txt, [4693s])
- A trade can be a good setup even if it loses, based on the information available at the time. "This looked like a really good trade, okay? (ffgXS_5BP9s_transcript.txt, [343s-365s])
