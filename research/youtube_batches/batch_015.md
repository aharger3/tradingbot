**3. 84% rule / re-entries:**

- There are two entry models for the 84% rule: the aggressive reclaim entry (buying as price breaks back above the level) and the retest entry (waiting for a pullback to the level). "There's two different entry models... you can wait for you look for the reclaim or you can wait for the potential retest either way works really, it's just situational." (lOKLMktXgY8_transcript.txt, [519s-530s])
- The majority of 84% rule trades are taken on the reclaim, not the retest. "Majority of my 84 percent rules are reclaimed... I'd say 80 percent of my trades are reclaimed and then 20 percent are the actual the retest because it doesn't always happen." (lOKLMktXgY8_transcript.txt, [885s-913s])
- For the 84% rule, you must wait for a candle to close above the key area; a wick above is not sufficient. "You have to wait for the candle to close above the key area for 84% rule.
