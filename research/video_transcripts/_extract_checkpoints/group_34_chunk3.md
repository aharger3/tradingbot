### Trade Review / KPI Tracking (Methodology)

- **Focus on what works, not just what's wrong** "there's people that they focus a lot on what they are doing wrong... But then sometimes they forget to also focus on what actually works" (performance-coaching_Session_8_KPI_Playbook_transcript.txt [172s-201s])
- **Identify patterns in best days AND worst days** "we will try to identify the patterns on your best days, but also in your worst days, and once we identify those patterns, then we can start fixing them" (performance-coaching_Session_8_KPI_Playbook_transcript.txt [321s-330s])
- **Shift mindset: you're generating data for your system, not just trading** "The moment you realize that you are not actually trading the trading front of you, but you are actually generating data for your system, everything changes... your commitment to create the best quality piece of data for your system" (performance-coaching_Session_8_KPI_Playbook_transcript.txt [1148s-1175s])
- **Execute for your system, not to make money** "You start executing or performing for your system instead for trying to win to make money on the trading front of you. You start just now trading a system like a machine... he knows exactly how his system behaves. He knows exactly what happens if he breaks the rules" (performance-coaching_Session_8_KPI_Playbook_transcript.txt [1182s-1226s])
- **Without following rules, data is unreliable** "if you don't follow your playbook, the data, it's not going to be reliable... you will never be able to say, okay, well, this strategy can hold a 15% drawdown. Why? Because you always are moving your stop loss, or because you are not respecting your stop loss." (performance-coaching_Session_8_KPI_Playbook_transcript.txt [1268s-1296s])
- **Your work is to create data, not make money on the next trade** "Your work now won't be, make money on the next trade. It's going to be create enough data for your system. So in the time, it will give you what you are looking, but the one requisite that is going to take from you to actually make money is that you actually follow the system" (performance-coaching_Session_8_KPI_Playbook_transcript.txt [1322s-1348s])
- **Measure trade quality on execution, not outcome** "we don't measure the quality of our trades on the outcome... we trade the quality of our trades on your performance on your execution... There could be a super nice trade that ended red why because you closed it right in your stop loss... that's a win in my books because you did everything right." (performance-coaching_Session_8_KPI_Playbook_transcript.txt [3112s-3167s])
- **Focus on performance, P&L follows** "if you start focusing on your performance and your execution, the outcome will follow... the moment you decide to be consistent with your execution... you will see the outcome will follow you" (performance-coaching_Session_8_KPI_Playbook_transcript.txt [3183s-3207s])
