## Why Traders Don't Follow Their Trading Plan

- **Reason 1: Previous stop-out that would have worked if held longer** "in previous history, they've seen that they've had a stop loss... And they did stop out of their trade in that previous trade. However, the second they stopped out of their trade, it went all the way back up to New High Day... And in their head, they're like, if I just didn't stop out for another minute or two, I would have made all that profit" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_6_Risk_Management_What_To_Focus_on_As_A_New_Trader_Pa_transcript.txt [800s-830s])

- **Reason 2: Previous trade where they ignored stop loss and won** "they didn't exit in their stop loss the first time... And they did win that trade... So they had a stop loss, they didn't exit. However, that trade went all the way back up to their profit target and they won. And now they're thinking they're a genius... And those two things are very, very, very dangerous" (mastermind-1-0_Lesson_6_Risk_Management_What_To_Focus_on_As_A_New_Trader_Pa_transcript.txt [840s-869s])
