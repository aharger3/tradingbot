- **Mistakes come from lack of readiness** "Most mistakes come from lack of readiness and not lack of skill." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [1112s-1121s])
- **P&L is a lagging indicator** "Your P&L, it's a lagging indicator of your progress." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [2676s-2688s])

### Three Levels of Preparation
- **Mental prep** "We have our mental prep. That is our mindset and energy." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [1323s-1334s])
- **Technical prep** "Having our key levels marked, having our high conviction setups or zones, planning for different scenarios." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [3015s-3030s])
- **Environmental prep** "Treat your trading space like a cockpit, nothing extra, nothing messy. Every second of your attention counts." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [3122s-3129s])

### Preparation vs. Prediction
- **Not predicting, reacting** "It's not about guessing. It's about reacting. You will always be better positioned to react to a scenario that you create, bear." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [3052s-3070s])
- **Plan for scenarios, not certainty** "Focus on different scenarios, not certain. You need to be okay with market uncertainty." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [4068s-4078s])
- **Preparation ≠ control** "Do not confuse preparation with control. Remember, market. I mean, you need to be fine with market uncertainty, right? So you are not trying to guess what will happen. You are preparing for how to respond when it does." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [3942s-3967s])

- **Routine signals game time** "Every time you see your routine or just start your routine, you know, it's game time. And that's how you reduce randomness." (performance-coaching_session-14-performance-through-preparation.txt [3372s-3383s])
