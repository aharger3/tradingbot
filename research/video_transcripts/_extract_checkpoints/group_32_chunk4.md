## ADDITIONAL METHODOLOGY RULES

### Growth vs. Fixed Mindset
- **[Growth mindset = emphasis on continuous learning, challenges as opportunities, setbacks as part of journey]** "emphasis on continuous learning... seeing challenges as growth opportunities... understanding that setbacks are part of the journey" (performance-coaching_Session_2_Performance_Mindset.txt [352s-375s])
- **[Fixed mindset = avoidance of risk, blaming external factors, stagnation]** "on the fixed mindset, it says drawbacks, avoidance of risk, blaming external factors, stagnation. Example, I'm not good at trading. The market is against me" (performance-coaching_Session_2_Performance_Mindset.txt [1768s-1783s])
- **[Fixed mindset goals focus on money outcomes; growth mindset goals focus on process improvement]** "if you set your goal with all that background, sounds amazing... the thing is, what happens if it's a sideways market? If you never hit your outcome goal, you are failing?" (performance-coaching_Session_2_Performance_Mindset.txt [1235s-1251s])
- **[Process-oriented goals vs. outcome-oriented goals]** "why instead of having a goal of making $500 a trade, why don't we say, I will have better entries? Or, I will scale out according to my plan. Or, I'm only taking trades that make sense on the risk reward ratio" (performance-coaching_Session_2_Performance_Mindset.txt [1086s-1099s])
- **[Money goals can drive decisions that backfire]** "fixing our goals to money not only can increase our emotions, but can drive our decisions on the money. And this can backfire in both sides... it can hold us from runners, from good winners because we are afraid of losing unrealized profit" (performance-coaching_Session_2_Performance_Mindset.txt [1669s-1698s])
- **[Self-reflection: how many of your trading rules are growth vs. fixed mindset]** "how many of your rules are growth mindset and how many of your rules are fixed mindset?" (performance-coaching_Session_2_Performance_Mindset.txt [1744s-1754s])
- **[Example of fixed mindset rule: stopping after loss out of fear vs. sticking to system]** "how many of you have a rule of I'm not going to make a second trade if I lost my first one?... And then you see that your setup actually shows it continues nicely. And you miss out out of fear, out of hesitation, out of a fixed mindset" (performance-coaching_Session_2_Performance_Mindset.txt [1806s-1859s])
- **[Growth mindset alternative for red folder news: stick to system with awareness]** "why don't say, on red folder news days, I'm going to stick one hundred percent to my system and I will be very aware of my risk and reactions" (performance-coaching_Session_2_Performance_Mindset.txt [1870s-1875s])
