## 1. Break-and-Retest

### Valid Break (Bodies vs Wicks, Displacement)
- **Candle closure above level required**: "it can't a closure above the level let's just call it that candle break a candle closure above level" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1056s-1062s])
- **Displacement candle must be fully outside the zone**: "This candle is fully outside of the zone... There's space here and the reason why I say this is not just for the confirmed break out" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1127s-1139s])
- **Without full break and closure outside level, no confirmed breakout**: "Until you have a full break in closure outside of the level you don't really have a confirmed break out" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1119s-1126s])
- **Displacement defined**: "All you all it is is essentially except you have a zone... you are looking for a full candle outside of it" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1751s-1760s])
- **Must have a down close candle to define the high**: "there has to be a down close candle... there has to be some sort of pivot high right can't just be green candles over and over and over again" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [893s-897s], [1029s-1034s])
- **First down close candle is where you start paying attention**: "this is like technically where you're starting to pay attention" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [842s-848s])

### Valid Retest (Touch/Zone, Max Wait)
- **Do NOT enter on the retest candle itself**: "Zero percent of the time you're gonna want to enter in on the retest candle... There is very little chance that you know exactly what's going to happen after you do have three confirmations" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1293s-1310s])
- **Decision must be made on the candle AFTER the retest**: "You have to make a decision on the next candle after the retest candle you have to make a decision here" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1362s-1373s])
- **Max candles from breakout to retest**: "Over eight candles form from candlestick one the three break it to retest" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [2989s-3000s])
- **Ideal is 3-8 candles from breakout to retest**: "three to eight candles from one... From candlestick one... Three or... Break out to retest... This is ideal" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [2939s-2972s])
- **Avoid immediate retests**: "just don't take immediate retests like you just basically displacement" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [4779s-4781s])

### Entry Trigger (Hammer/Shooting Star)
- **Entry on candle 5 (after retest + confirmation)**: "you press buy when this fifth candle... starts forming right above this fourth candle" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1846s-1857s])
- **Alternative: enter on candle 4 if it breaks above candle 3**: "you can enter on the confirmation candle technically... if you decide to enter in on this fourth candle... it needs to break above" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1539s-1554s])
- **Wait 45-60 seconds before entering**: "you probably want to wait at least 45 to 60 seconds here... either waiting for the close or waiting for 45 seconds passed just so it doesn't reverse on you" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1425s-1434s], [1546s-1554s])
- **Entry trigger: candle 5 takes out previous candle high**: "Candle five takes out the previous candle already after ten set yes 100%... As soon as that pops" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1885s-1895s])
- **Sniper entry on inside bar/squeeze**: "a little bit of a squeeze or an inside bar... right here... You were getting another... Good opportunity for a sniper entry is what I would call this" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1457s-1472s])
- **Color of retest candle doesn't matter**: "it doesn't really matter what color it doesn't matter if it's a down close at our close candle... the only thing that matters is where the top of that candle is and what came before it" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [3751s-3760s])

### Stop Placement
- **Stop loss should be the retest candle low**: "the stop loss should just be the time to close stop right the the higher low... It should just be the retest candle night" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1833s-1840s])

### Targets (HOD/LOD -> Liquidity)
- **Target is next liquidity/sellers**: "If we can break above this pivot intro day... we know at least... We're gonna we're gonna probably hit high of day" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [2148s-2162s])
- **Price gravitates to next point where sellers are**: "It's gonna gravitate towards this high up here... Because that is the next point where sellers are" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [2098s-2106s])
- **Logic: break above sell orders takes out next HTF target**: "If structure can hold and we can break above... Those orders we are typically going to take out a next... Higher time frame target" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1972s-1983s])

### First-Break vs Late Breaks
- **Best trades are off HOD/LOD**: "Every single time the best trade is gonna be off high day... it's because typically the structures just intact always you're never trading to the wrong side" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [594s-604s])
- **If price shoots up too far from level, don't enter**: "if you are waiting for this candle to form the fourth candle and it shoots up right away... You're not entering this trade anymore" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1586s-1591s])
- **If R:R below 1:1, don't enter**: "Depending on if you were trade is above a one to one risk or award... You are no longer going to enter this trade" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [1610s-1621s])
- **If HTF objective already hit, don't take the retest**: "if this are it's this higher to get's taken out and you see a pullback... You're essentially knocking along anymore you're not gonna buy down here it already hit the main higher time frame objective" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [3099s-3112s])

### Large Upper Wick on Breakout/Displacement Candle
- **Be careful of large upper wick on breakout/displacement candle**: "If breakout slash displacement candle has large upper... lower... reasons... be careful of that breakout displacement count all right like look logically at the chart" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [2707s-2751s])

### Structure Intact Requirement
- **Cannot have structure shift/change of character**: "you can't have a set up where okay you reject and then you have like something like this... This is just a no go... trend shifts on the low-time frames" (hayden-s-coaching_How_to_enter_the_break_and_retest_trade.txt [2882s-2901s])
