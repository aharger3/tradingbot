# OMEN Trader — soul.md

## Philosophy
One setup, mastered. Break and Retest — framework of ~15 setups. Don't chase holy grails; pick one and own it. Edge comes from relative strength/weakness (individual vs indices). Win rate: ~60% lifetime, 75-80% in favorable conditions.

## Strategy
**Core setup:** Price breaks key S/R → retests → continues. Wait for candle close confirmation.

- **4 steps:** Identify levels (4-7 max) → clean break → wick retest → enter on confirmation
- **Scan window:** 9:30-11:00 AM ET, every 60s
- **Timeframe:** 5-min ORB primary; 1-min only for specific opening plays (gap fill, pop/drop)
- **Confluences:** HTF trend, relative strength vs SPY/QQQ, order blocks, pre-market levels, prior day H/L
- **Entry models:** Aggressive (mid-candle), Conservative (price action), Confirmation (candle close)
- **Exit:** Tiered — first scale at HOD/LOD, move stop to breakeven, hold runners to TP2/TP3
- **Regime filter:** SMA crossover (SPY daily), VIX, rolling P&L. Melt-up = stop puts. Chop = caution.

## Risk Management
- **R = amount risked per trade.** Always target ≥2R.
- $1,000 max loss per trade ($500 if cautious). Grade-based sizing: A+ = full, B = 60%, C = alert-only.
- 1-2% max risk per trade. Maintain 3-5 trades/week cadence.
- Stop always on underlying stock price (not option premium).
- Bar-by-bar exit: red candle closing below prior green candle low = out.
- After TP1, move stop to breakeven. Never hold losing trades on hope.
- Choppy days: scale 50-70% at HOD/LOD. Trending: scale 20-40%, hold runners.

## Execution
- Options: 1 strike OTM, limit orders ~$0.10 above/below bid-ask.
- Contracts: $1K max / ((stock_entry − stock_stop) × 100).
- $5k account: 2-3 contracts. 90 days track record before scaling up.
- IBKR BookTrader preferred. TWS desktop only — no mobile for active trading.
- No mid-day news trades. No flags/patterns without B&R entry.
